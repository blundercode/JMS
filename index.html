Who We Are & What We Do

We are a small team dedicated to giving everyone the abilty to have a website.

In today's world everyone needs to have a presence on the web. Whether your finding a job, applying for an internship or trying to get into college you need something to set you apart in todays competitive market. According to Forbes 56% of all hiring managers are more impressed by a candidate’s personal website than any other personal branding tool—however, only 7% of job seekers actually have a personal website. We are determined to change this. JumpstartPages understands how daunting a task it can be to undertake making a website. Thats why we take care of everything from making the site, to purchasing a domain, to hosting, all you have to do is fill out a short form, we'll take care of the rest.
